# Baby Bok Choy And Shiitake Stir Fry

## Ingredients

* 1 tbsp peanut oil  
* Â½ lb shiitake mushrooms, stems removed, caps sliced  
* 2 garlic cloves, grated  
* 2 tsp ginger, grated  
* 1 to 2 small dried red chiles, seeds, stems removed, cut into 1/2-inch pieces  
* Kung Pao sauce  
  * 2 Tbsp shaoxing wine (sub sake)  
  * 2 Tbsp chinkiang black rice vinegar  
  * 1 Tbsp soy sauce (sub tamari)  
  * 1 Tbsp mirin (sub 2 tsp sugar)  
  * 1 tsp sesame oil  
* 1 lb baby bok choy, leaves separated from stalks, rinsed  
* Salt, sichuan pepper, chili oil to taste  
* Scallions, sesame seeds garnish

## Directions

* Fry garlic, ginger, chiles, Sichuan, mushrooms in oil on medium-high  
* Deglaze with sauce  
* Stir in bok choy, until wilted  
* Season with salt, sichuan pepper, chili oil  
* Serve over white rice, garnish with scallions and toasted sesame seeds
