# Baby Bok Choy And Mushroom Stir Fry

*Originally learned from a recipe distributed by the local CSA farm as a use for seasonal baby bok choy and mushrooms.*

## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp peanut oil  
- Â½ lb mushrooms, sliced (portobello are good, shiitake better, but any will do)
- 2 garlic cloves, grated  
- 2 tsp ginger, grated  
- 1 to 2 small dried red chiles, seeds, stems removed, cut into 1/2-inch pieces  
- Kung Pao sauce:
  - 2 tbsp shaoxing wine (sub sake)  
  - 2 tbsp chinkiang black rice vinegar  
  - 1 tbsp soy sauce (sub tamari)  
  - 1 tbsp mirin (sub 2 tsp sugar)  
  - 1 tsp sesame oil  
- 1 lb baby bok choy, leaves separated from stalks, rinsed  
- Salt, sichuan pepper, chili oil to taste

### For Garnish

- Scallions, sliced
- Sesame seeds, toasted

## Instructionsns

1. Fry garlic, ginger, chiles, Sichuan, mushrooms in oil on medium-high  
2. Deglaze with sauce  
3. Stir in bok choy, until wilted  
4. Season with salt, sichuan pepper, chili oil  
5. Serve over white rice with garnishes
