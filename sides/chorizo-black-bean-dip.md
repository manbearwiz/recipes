# Chorizo Black Bean Dip

Attempt at replicating the complimentary chorizo black bean dip from Antojitos in Appleton, WI.

## Ingredients

- 6 oz Mexican-style chorizo, crumbled  
- 1 lb dried black beans  
- 1 medium onion, split  
- 6 whole garlic cloves  
- 1/2 orange, split in half  
- 2 bay leaves  
- 1L chicken broth (or water + 1 Tbsp Better Than Bouillon)

## Instructions

1. Fry chorizo in a pressure cooker over medium heat, until crisp (~2 min)  
2. Add remaining ingredients
3. Lock lid, cook on high pressure for 45 minutes, then natural release after 15 min
4. Discard onion, orange, bay leaves  
5. Optionally, mash some of the beans or puree very briefly with an immersion blender
6. Season to taste with salt and serve
