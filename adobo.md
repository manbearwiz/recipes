# Adobo

Smoky, tangy sauce base for many Mexican dishes like Tacos Al Pastor, Pozole Rojo, and Chili Con Carne.

## Ingredients

- Dried Chilies (stems and seeds removed):
  - 6 dried ancho chilies
  - 6 dried cascabel chilies
  - 6 dried New Mexico chilies
- 500 ml chicken stock (or water + 1 Tbsp bouillon)
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 3 onions, ~400g, diced
- 12 garlic cloves, minced
- 1/4 cup tomato paste
- Spices:
  - 2 tbsp whole cumin seeds
  - 4 tsp whole Mexican oregano
  - 1 tsp whole black peppercorns
- 1/4 tablet (23g) Nestlé Abuelita Mexican Hot Chocolate
- 100 ml apple cider vinegar

## Instructions

1. **Chiles:** Remove stems/seeds. Microwave 20–30 seconds. Simmer in stock until soft.
2. **Aromatics:** Sauté onions 5–7 min. Add garlic, cumin, oregano, peppercorns; cook 1 min.
3. **Combine:** Add chiles with liquid, tomato paste, chocolate, vinegar. Simmer 10 min.
4. **Blend:** Dilute to 1.2 liters. Blend smooth. Salt to taste.
5. **Store:** Cool, portion into 8 150ml servings and freeze.

## Quick Chili Con Carne

- 1 lb ground beef
- 300ml adobo sauce
- 500ml chicken broth (or water + bouillon)
- 1 can (15 oz/425 g) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
- Serve with sour cream, tortilla chips

Brown beef in a dutch oven. Add remaining ingredients. Simmer 15 min. Salt to taste. Serve with sour cream and chips.

## Tacos Al Pastor

- 2 lbs (900 g) pork butt, sliced into thin strips
- 150ml adobo sauce
- 1 small pineapple, cut in chunks
- Serve with corn tortillas, cilantro, lime, salsa

Marinate pork in sauce 4–36 hours. Skewer with pineapple, grill 8–10 min. Cut pork and pineapple off skewers. Serve on tortillas with toppings.

## Caribbean Beans and Rice

- 1.5L stock (or water + 2 Tbsp bouillon)
- 3 bay leaves
- 450g dried black beans, rinsed
- 450g brown rice, rinsed
- 150ml adobo sauce
- Serve with sour cream, tortilla chips

Pressure cook all ingredients 25 min, natural release 15 min. Remove bay leaves. Serve with sour cream and chips.

## Pozole Rojo de Pollo

- 1 rotisserie chicken, shredded (~600g)
- 1 can (25oz/700g) hominy, drained
- 150ml adobo sauce
- 1L chicken stock
- Serve with cabbage, radishes, lime, onion, cilantro, avocado, tostadas

Combine all ingredients in pot and simmer 10 min. Salt to taste. Serve with garnishes.
