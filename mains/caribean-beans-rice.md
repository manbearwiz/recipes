# Pressure Cooker Cuban Black Beans And Rice (Moros y Cristianos)

## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp coconut oil
- 150ml [adobo sauce](../sauces/adobo.md) (or use aromatics below)
- Aromatics (if not using adobo):
  - 1 large onion, finely chopped
  - 6 cloves garlic, minced or grated  
  - 1 tsp dried mexican oregano  
  - 1 tsp ground cumin seed  
  - 2 dried red chilies (e.g. cascabel, new mexico), stems and seeds removed  
- 1.6L stock (or water + 2 tbsp bouillon)  
- 3 bay leaves  
- 450g dried black beans, rinsed and picked over  
- 450g brown rice, rinsed  
- hot sauce to taste (e.g. valentina, cholula)

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in pressure cooker
2. Add adobo if using, otherwise add onion, cook until soft (~5 min), then remaining aromatics cook until fragrant (~1 min)
3. Add stock, bay leaves, and beans - stir and scrape up any browned bits
4. Once boiling, add rice and stir
5. Lock lid, cook on high pressure for 25 min, then natural release after 15 min
6. Remove chilies and bay leaves
7. Stir well, adjust seasoning with salt and hot sauce to taste
