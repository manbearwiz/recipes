# Red Beans and Rice

## Ingredients

- 1 lb dried kidney beans, rinsed and picked over
- 1 lb andouille sausage (4 links), sliced
- 1 lb smoked ham shank
- Trinity (the "holy trinity" of Cajun cooking):
  - 2 medium onions, diced
  - 1 medium green bell pepper, diced
  - 2 celery stalks, diced
- Aromatics:
  - 5 garlic cloves, minced
  - 3 bay leaves
  - 4 sprigs fresh thyme (or 1 tsp dried)
  - 4 mixed dried red chilies (e.g. new mexico, cascabel), stems and seeds removed
- 5 cups water
- Salt, pepper, hot sauce, apple cider vinegar to taste

### For Garnish

- Scallions, sliced
- Fresh parsley, chopped

## Instructions

1. Brown andouille in pressure cooker over medium-high heat (~5 min).
2. Add trinity. Cook until softened (~5 min).
3. Add aromatics. Cook until fragrant (~1 min).
4. Add beans, ham shank, and water. Stir to combine.
5. Pressure cook on high for 45 minutes, then natural release after 15 minutes.
6. Remove bay leaves and ham bone. Shred any meat from the shank and return to pot.
7. Mash some beans against the side of the pot to thicken.
8. Season with salt, pepper, hot sauce, and vinegar to taste.
9. Serve over rice with garnishes.
