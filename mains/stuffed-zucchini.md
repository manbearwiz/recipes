# Calabacitas Rellenas (Mexican Stuffed Zucchini)

## Ingredients

- 4 medium zucchini, halved, seeded
- 2 tbsp tallow (or oil)
- 1 white onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 cup white rice, rinsed
- ½ tbsp ground cumin
- 1 small dried hot chili, seeded and chopped
- 1.5 cups chicken broth (or water + 1 tsp bouillon)
- 1 cup Cotija or Queso Fresco, crumbled

### For Garnish

- Limes
- Cilantro
- Sour cream
- Hot sauce

## Instructions

1. Score the zucchini flesh, sprinkle with salt, and let sit for 30 minutes to draw out moisture.
2. Preheat oven to 450°F (use convection if available).
3. Heat oil in a medium pot over medium heat.
4. Add onion and cook until softened, 5 minutes
5. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, 1 minute
6. Stir in rice, cumin, and chili. Toast until translucent (~2 min).
7. Add broth, bring to a boil, then cover and simmer on low for 15 minutes.
8. Pat zucchini dry, rub with oil, and arrange cut-side down on a sheet pan.
9. Roast on the top rack until browned and tender, 15–20 minutes.
10. Set oven to Broil.
11. Flip zucchini, fill with the rice mixture, and top with cheese.
12. Broil until cheese is melted and golden (~2 minutes). Watch carefully.
13. Serve with garnishes.
