# Pressure Cooker "Hot Beast" (Shredded Venison Sandwiches)

## Ingredients

* 900g (2 lb) venison roast, trimmed and cut into 1–2" cubes
* 2 tbsp beef tallow or oil
* 1 large onion, coarsely chopped
* 4 cloves garlic, crushed
* 2 tbsp tomato paste
* ~100ml dry red wine, vermouth, or beer
* 350ml chicken broth (or water + 1 tsp bouillon)
* 2 tbsp umami sauce (Worcestershire, fish sauce, soy, etc.)
* 1 tbsp sweet Hungarian paprika
* 2 bay leaves
* Salt and pepper
* 2 tbsp cornstarch + 2 tbsp cold water
* Soft buns

## Instructions

1. Heat tallow in pressure cooker. Season venison with salt and pepper and brown 5–7 min; work in batches if crowded.
2. Add onion and cook ~3 min. Add garlic and tomato paste; cook ~1 min.
3. Deglaze with alcohol and scrape up bits. Stir in broth, umami, paprika, and bay leaves.
4. Pressure cook on *High* for **40 min**, natural release **15 min**.
5. Remove bay leaves. Shred meat in the pot. Stir in cornstarch slurry and simmer 1–2 min to thicken.
6. Serve hot on buns.
