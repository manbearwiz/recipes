# Pressure Cooker Cuban Black Beans And Rice (Moros y Cristianos)

## Ingredients

* 1 tbsp olive oil  
* 1 large onion, finely chopped  
* 6 cloves garlic, minced or grated  
* 1 tsp dried oregano  
* 1 tsp ground cumin seed  
* 2 dried red chilies (e.g. cascabel, new mexico), stems and seeds removed  
* 1.5L stock (or water \+ 2 tbsp bouillon)  
* 3 bay leaves  
* 450g dried black beans, rinsed and picked over  
* 450g brown rice, rinsed  
* 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in pressure cooker on saut√© mode  
2. Add onion, cook until soft (\~5 min)  
3. Add garlic, oregano, cumin, and chilies, cook until fragrant (\~1 min)  
4. Add stock, bay leaves, beans, and rice, stir and scrape up browned bits  
5. Lock lid, cook on high pressure for 25 min, then natural release after 10 min  
6. Remove chilies and bay leaves  
7. Stir in vinegar and season to taste with salt and pepper
