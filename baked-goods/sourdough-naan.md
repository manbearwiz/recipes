# Sourdough Naan

Great use for subpar sourdough starter discard.

## Ingredients

- 280g all-purpose flour (plus more as needed)
- 6g salt
- 5g sugar
- 200g sourdough starter
- 200g kefir or yogurt

### Optional Additions

- Fresh ginger, grated
- Garlic, minced

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients in a mixer with dough hook on low speed.
2. Knead 5 minutes. Depending on flour and age of starter, may need more flour to achieve workable dough consistency.
3. Cover and ferment 4-8 hours at room temperature, or days in the fridge.
4. Knead in optional ingredients (ginger, garlic) until distributed.
5. Divide into 8 balls, rest 10 minutes.
6. Stretch each ball and cook on medium-high heat ~2 minutes per side until puffed and charred.
