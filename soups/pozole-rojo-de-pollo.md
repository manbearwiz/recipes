# Pozole Rojo de Pollo

A traditional Mexican soup with shredded chicken, hominy, and a rich adobo-based broth.

## Ingredients

- 1 rotisserie chicken, shredded (~600g)
- 1 can (25oz/700g) hominy, drained
- 150ml [adobo sauce](../sauces/adobo.md)
- 1L chicken stock (or water + 1 tbsp bouillon)

### For Garnish

- Shredded cabbage
- Sliced radishes
- Lime wedges
- Diced white onion
- Fresh cilantro
- Sliced avocado
- Tostadas

## Instructions

1. Combine chicken, hominy, adobo sauce, and chicken stock in a large pot.
2. Bring to a simmer over medium heat and cook for 10 minutes.
3. Taste and adjust seasoning with salt.
4. Serve hot in bowls with garnishes on the side, allowing everyone to customize their bowl.
