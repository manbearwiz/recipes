# Italian Creamy Chicken Soup

## Ingredients

- 1 rotisserie chicken
  - \~600g cooked shredded chicken
- 800g soffritto
  - \~3 onion, \~400g, finely diced
  - \~3 carrot, \~200g, finely diced
  - \~5 celery ribs, \~200g, finely diced
- 6 cloves garlic, crushed
- 4 sprigs fresh herbs (rosemary, thyme, sage)
- 100ml dry boxed white wine
- 1l chicken broth (1l water \+ chicken carcass and/or better than bouillon)
- Bechamel (optional)
  - 56g (4 tbsp) butter
  - 56g flour
  - 500ml milk
  - 1/8 tsp nutmeg, grated
- Beans (optional)
  - 450g dried great northern beans
  - 1 bay leaf
- 16 oz mini potato gnocchi (optional)
- spinach or kale, fresh or frozen, chopped
- 1 cup heavy cream (optional)
- Shredded parmesan cheese, for serving (optional)

## Instructions

1. Remove meat from chicken carcass, pressure cook carcass 1hr, or boil 4hr
2. If using beans,
   1. Strain carcass from pressure cooker
   2. Pressure cook beans, broth, bay leaf, herbs, garlic, s\&p, 25 min
   3. Naturally release 15 min
   4. Strain and reserve half beans, immersion blend the rest, recombine
3. Heat a glug of evoo in dutch oven over med-high
4. Saute soffritto, s\&p, until tender & starting to caramelize, \~8 min
5. If not using beans, add garlic, herbs to dutch oven, saut√© \~1 min
6. Deglaze with wine, then broth, s\&p to taste, simmer
7. If making a bechamel,
   1. Melt butter in small saucepan over med until mostly done bubbling
   2. Add flour, whisk \~8 min until color of pb
   3. Add milk in batches, whisking lumps, simmer
   4. Season with nutmeg, s\&p to taste
8. Add greens to broth mixture, cook until wilted, \~1 min for spinach, \~10min for kale
9. If using gnocchi, add to broth mixture, cover and simmer for time on the package
10. Add thickener, chicken, optional heavy cream to dutch oven
11. Serve warm, garnish with parmesan
